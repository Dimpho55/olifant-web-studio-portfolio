Our Story

NEWLY SLY was born in Seshego out of a love for bold colours and comfortable fits. We blend modern streetwear sensibilities with locally inspired prints and materials — producing limited drops that celebrate individuality and pride in African design.

Each piece is designed with attention to fit and longevity; we partner with small local workshops to produce limited runs, keeping quality high and waste low. NEWLY SLY is for people who want statement pieces that last.
