Company Overview

Established in June 2015

Who We Are

REGCORP PTY LTD is a South African Emerging Multi-Discipline Company dedicated to delivering world-class services in the Technology sector to all our clients. We create job opportunities, particularly in previously disadvantaged communities, contributing meaningfully to the South African economy.

Our People

Our company prides itself in only employing highly skilled personnel with the right business acumen. This enables us to complete all projects timeously while transferring necessary skills to community members employed on our projects.

Geographical Footprint

REGCORP PTY LTD has established its footprint across South Africa and can execute any project or assignment in any of the nine provinces. Our national reach ensures consistent, reliable service delivery wherever our clients need us.

Business Diversity

As a true multi-disciplinary company, we operate across many spheres of business including Sales, Distribution, and Service in the Information Technology Sector, Food & Beverages sector, and supply of essential equipment and PPE.
