About REGCORP

A South African Legacy of Excellence

Company Overview

REGCORP PTY LTD is a South African Emerging Multi-Discipline Company established in June 2015. The company was formed with the sole purpose of delivering world class service in the Technology sector to all its clients thereby creating job opportunities mostly in previously disadvantaged communities and thus contributing to the South African Economy.

The diversity of the company and its directors enables us to tackle various commodity groups as we aim to play a part in eradicating unemployment to the Youth in our society and community.

Our Commitment

Our Company prides itself in only employing highly skilled personnel with the right business acumen in order to enable us to complete all projects timeously and thereby transferring the necessary skills to the community members employed on those projects.

Geographical Footprint

REGCORP PTY LTD has established its footprint and can execute any project or assignment in any of the nine provinces in South Africa.

Vision

To become a Company that is able to provide Turnkey Technology services that exceed industry standards and create lasting value for our clients and communities.

Mission

We aim to provide world class Technology service that by far exceed our client expectations, while driving sustainable growth and empowering local communities through skills transfer and employment opportunities.
